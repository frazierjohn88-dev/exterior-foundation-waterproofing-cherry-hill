Understanding Exterior Grading and Its Impact on Your Foundation

Exterior grading refers to the slope of the ground around your home's foundation. Proper grading ensures that water flows away from your foundation walls, while poor grading can direct water toward your home, leading to basement flooding and foundation damage.

Key Benefits of Proper Exterior Grading:

  • Prevents water accumulation near foundation walls
  • Reduces hydrostatic pressure on basement walls
  • Minimizes the risk of foundation cracks and deterioration
  • Protects your basement waterproofing system
  • Extends the lifespan of your foundation

Signs Your Property Needs Grading Improvement

If you notice any of these warning signs, it's time to consider our exterior grading and landscaping services:

  • Standing water near your foundation after rain
  • Basement water seepage or flooding
  • Soil erosion around your home
  • Foundation cracks requiring professional repair
  • Basement humidity issues
